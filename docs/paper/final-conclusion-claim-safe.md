# Final Conclusion Draft: Claim-Safe

Status: POST-8 submission package
Claim ceiling: `operational_utility_only/no_claim_upgrade`

This work frames context projection as dispatch-time evidence selection under
explicit claim gates. Its main contribution is a fail-closed, replayable,
claim-gated operational audit framework that connects formal V-information
motivation to backend-constrained operational evidence without overstating the
empirical package. The live-API-only setting is not treated as a shortcut to
stronger empirical claims: generated-token chat logprobs are not relabeled as
teacher-forced NLL or fixed-target continuation scores, LLM judges and
EPF-FINAL silver labels remain weak or candidate sources, and replayable
artifacts remain audit interfaces rather than measurement proof.

The present evidence is operational rather than validating. The contribution is
a replayable, claim-gated operational audit framework for dispatch-time
evidence projection under live-API constraints.

The resulting submission is deliberately conservative. It permits scoped
operational evaluation and weak-evidence diagnostics, while preserving the
negative bridge results and backend limitations that block stronger claims.
The paper does not assert deployed V-information claims, metric support,
formal proxy support, human/external measurement claims, selection-quality
dominance, Route 5 unlock, or Route 8 unlock. Future work can target those
routes only with the missing fixed-target scoring, human/external evidence,
and bridge evidence that the current package explicitly lacks.
