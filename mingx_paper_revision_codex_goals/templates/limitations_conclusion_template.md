# Limitations / Conclusion Template

Conclusion pattern:

> The present evidence is operational rather than validating. Under the supported live API, we do not establish fixed-target teacher-forced scoring, a utility-to-log-loss metric bridge, V-information proxy support, human/external measurement validation, or selector superiority. Instead, we contribute a replayable, claim-gated operational audit framework that exposes when dispatch-time evidence projection helps, when evidence is insufficient, when abstention or reprojection is appropriate, and how those outcomes can be inspected without storing raw API responses.
